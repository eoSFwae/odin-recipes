Hearty Beef Stew

Description

The most heart comfy stew you've ever made

Ingredients

  • beef
  • potatoes
  • carrots
  • chicken broth
  • onions

Steps

  1. cut all vegetables
  2. sear beef until golden brown
  3. fill pot with broth and bring to a boil
  4. put all remaining ingredients in the pot
  5. lower to a simmer and cook for 4 hours
